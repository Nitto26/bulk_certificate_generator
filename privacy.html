Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the bulkcertificategenerator application collects, uses, and shares your data when you visit and use our services.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We believe in absolute data privacy. Our application operates entirely client-side in your browser:

  • Spreadsheet & Template Data: Any Excel/CSV sheets and template images you import are parsed and cached locally in your browser's offline storage (IndexedDB). We do not send, transmit, or store this data on any remote server.
  • Email Cache: Base64 data from generated certificates is kept temporarily in local memory to attach them to your emails. This data is cleared when you close the tab or reset your workspace.

2. Google User Data (Gmail API)

Our application uses Google OAuth 2.0 to connect to your Gmail account and send certificates. This access is governed strictly by the following boundaries:

  • Purpose: Access is requested solely for the purpose of sending bulk certificates to your participants.
  • Scopes Used: We request the gmail.send scope (to transmit emails with attachments) and userinfo.email (to identify your sender address).
  • Data Sharing: Your Google authentication tokens and profile data live inside your browser session and are never shared with us or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Cloud Services (identity tokens and Gmail REST APIs) to complete the mailout. The usage of this data is subject to Google's Privacy Policies.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy statement, you can reach out via our GitHub profile: github.com/Nitto26.
